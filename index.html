About Lori

Your Partner in Health & Wellness

With over 16 years of diverse healthcare experience, Lori Ursu brings a unique combination of perioperative nursing expertise, emergency care knowledge, and holistic wellness practices to provide comprehensive patient care.

Her extensive background spans perioperative nursing, emergency room care, and reflexology therapy. Lori believes in treating the whole person through evidence-based medical care combined with natural wellness approaches to promote healing and optimal health.
